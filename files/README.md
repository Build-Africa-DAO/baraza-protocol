# 🏛️ Baraza

**African cooperative infrastructure on Solana.**

*Baraza (Swahili) — the traditional community council. The place where decisions are made.*

RSVP with your wallet → Own your ticket as an NFT → Pay with M-Pesa → Launch your community DAO → Govern your treasury.
