# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Baraza serves African savings groups, SACCOs, community collectives, and their organizers. Members need to contribute, understand shared rules, vote, and verify treasury activity from a phone without learning blockchain concepts.

## Product Purpose

Baraza turns a community's plain-language operating rules into a transparent, non-custodial treasury and governance workflow. Success means every member can see the same source of truth while the infrastructure stays out of the way.

## Brand Personality

Warm, accountable, and capable. Baraza should feel familiar enough for a community meeting and rigorous enough for shared money.

## Anti-references

Avoid crypto-trading aesthetics, chain-first jargon, generic enterprise dashboards, speculative token marketing, and decorative landing-page patterns that make the product feel less trustworthy or less local.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the community task, not the underlying infrastructure.
- Make shared money and decisions easy to inspect.
- Use familiar language for contributions, rules, and participation.
- Keep first-run paths short, phone-friendly, and confidence-building.
- Show working product states instead of abstract technology claims.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, reduced motion, readable contrast, and layouts that remain usable on small and low-cost mobile screens.
